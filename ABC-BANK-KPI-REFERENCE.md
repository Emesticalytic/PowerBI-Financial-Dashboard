## Calculation Notes

### Gross Margin Formula
```
Gross Margin % = (Total Revenue - Total COGS) / Total Revenue * 100
```

### YoY Growth Formula
```
YoY Growth % = (Current Year Revenue - Prior Year Revenue) / Prior Year Revenue * 100
```

### Opportunity Score (0-100 scale)
Based on:
- Market size
- Growth rate
